SEND Compass
Find the right support for your child, wherever you are.
SEND stands for Special Educational Needs and Disabilities — the term used across many international school systems worldwide. In the UAE, this is also known with pride as support for People of Determination. Whatever the words used where you are, this tool is here to help you find the right centre, school, or specialist support for your child.
GlobalCompass doesn't diagnose or assess children. This tool helps you find support by what you're looking for — for a formal diagnosis, please consult a licensed specialist.

It's okay not to know yet. Many parents notice something first, often around communication or language, long before any formal assessment. Trusting what you've noticed is a completely valid place to start.

If you haven't had a formal assessment yet, a good first step in the UAE is speaking with your child's paediatrician or school, who can refer you to a licensed specialist for evaluation.
