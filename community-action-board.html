🇰🇪 Rural Kenya — Africa
Build a Classroom — Rift Valley Schools Project
Organised by: Bridges of Hope Kenya
A two-week residential programme helping build and paint classrooms for primary schools in the Rift Valley region. Students work alongside local builders, contribute to fundraising before travel, and run a one-day reading event for local children on the final day. One of the most complete CAS project experiences available globally — covering Action, Creativity, and Service in a single project.
