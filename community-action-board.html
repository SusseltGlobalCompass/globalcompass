Community Action Board

Real community projects for students who want to do something that matters — and count their hours toward CAS, Duke of Edinburgh, VCE, and other service requirements at the same time.

Every project listed here has been reviewed by the GlobalCompass team before appearing.
Gulf and Middle East
Dubai, UAE
Beach Clean and Marine Awareness Day
Organised by: EcoSchools UAE Network
Join a monthly beach clean along Jumeirah Beach, followed by a short awareness session on marine plastic pollution and its effect on the Gulf's coral reefs. Students also help create bilingual awareness materials distributed to local families.
Environment 3-5 hours per session IB CAS · DofE
Monthly — last Saturday
Abu Dhabi, UAE
Literacy Tutoring for Migrant Children
Organised by: Gulf Education Volunteers
Volunteer tutors help children of migrant workers (ages 6-12) with basic English and mathematics literacy in weekend sessions. No teaching experience needed — training is provided. One of the most meaningful CAS projects available in the UAE.
Education 4 hours per week IB CAS · A-Levels
Weekends year-round
Africa
Rural Kenya
Build a Classroom — Rift Valley Schools Project
Organised by: Bridges of Hope Kenya
A two-week residential programme helping build and paint classrooms for primary schools in the Rift Valley region. Students work alongside local builders, contribute to fundraising before travel, and run a one-day reading event for local children on the final day.
Construction 80+ hours IB CAS · DofE Gold · VCE
July and December cohorts
Cape Town, South Africa
Girls' Education Campaign — Township Schools
Organised by: She Leads Africa Foundation
Create and deliver age-appropriate educational workshops on career pathways and digital literacy for girls aged 12-16 in Cape Town township schools. Students design their own session with support from the foundation's education team.
Education 20-40 hours IB CAS · CBSE
Term-time and holidays
Asia Pacific
Bali, Indonesia
Reef Restoration and Ocean Education
Organised by: Coral Triangle Initiative
Work with marine biologists to plant coral fragments on degraded reef sections off Bali's north coast. Students also create illustrated guides explaining reef ecology, distributed to local fishing communities in Indonesian and English.
Environment 30-60 hours IB CAS · Singapore · VCE
June and November cohorts
Rural India
Digital Literacy for Village Schools
Organised by: Teach for Rural India
Train primary school teachers in rural Rajasthan to use basic tablet technology in their classrooms. Students develop short training sessions, deliver them in person or remotely, and follow up with resource packs tailored to each school's needs.
Education 40+ hours IB CAS · CBSE · DofE
Year-round with remote option
Americas
São Paulo, Brazil
Reading Circles in Favela Communities
Organised by: Leitura Viva Brasil
Run weekly reading sessions for children aged 5-10 in community centres across São Paulo's peripheral neighbourhoods. Students choose and adapt books, create simple activities, and keep a reflective journal — a natural fit for ENEM students and strong CAS evidence.
Education 3 hours per week IB CAS · ENEM · French Bac
School term weeks
Medellín, Colombia
Urban Garden and Food Security Project
Organised by: Huerta Comunitaria Medellín
Help design and maintain rooftop vegetable gardens in low-income neighbourhoods, with harvests donated to local families. Students also run nutrition workshops and document the project through photography and written reports.
Environment 20-50 hours IB CAS · DofE · ENEM
Ongoing — flexible commitment
