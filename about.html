About Us
Our story
One home for every step of a child's education journey, worldwide.
GlobalCompass exists to give expat and internationally mobile families a single, trustworthy guide — from the first nursery search to the final university pathway.
Why we exist
The gap we saw firsthand

Expat and internationally mobile families face a genuinely hard problem: choosing the right school, the right curriculum, and the right pathway for their child, often while adjusting to a completely new country and system at the same time. Without a single trustworthy guide, this becomes a confusing, expensive process built on scattered forums, word of mouth, and guesswork.

GlobalCompass was built to close that gap — starting in the UAE, with real, verified data, and a genuine intent to grow honestly, market by market, worldwide.

Our founder
Built from lived experience, not assumption

GlobalCompass is built by Susselt, a former IB teacher and mother, based in Dubai. It's a genuinely personal project — developed hands-on, with AI assistance, despite no formal technical background.

"I saw, as a teacher and as a mother, how much confusion expat families carry — not because they don't care, but because no single place ever explained it all clearly. GlobalCompass is the resource I wished existed."
What makes us different
A real bridge, not just a directory

GlobalCompass isn't only a school-search engine. Its Curriculum Guides, Curriculum Quiz, and Track My Scores work together to form something genuinely rare: a real bridge from choosing a curriculum, all the way through to understanding the right university and career pathway — built specifically for internationally mobile families navigating final years across different systems.
